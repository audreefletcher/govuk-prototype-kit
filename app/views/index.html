About

This work is led by the DDaT heads of profession, who help DfE service owners and their teams create and run great services that meet government standards.

This website is the place where DDaT delivery teams can find and publish information on building and running services at DfE.

How to publish to the Service Delivery Manual

Your content must:

  • have a valid user need
  • be approved by a head of profession
  • have an owner who will keep the content up to date and be a point of contact

A content designer will work with you to get your content in the right place and in the right style.
