Transformation aims

This is "for now" text. The page will be designed by Head of Transformation in collaboration with the DDaT and service owner communities.

When you join us

The DfE senior leadership challenged us to become more delivery-focused as a department.

We need to:

  • Become an organisation that knows how to deliver and enable excellent, joined-up public services
  • Connect the work we do more closely to desird policy, user and provider outcomes
  • Focus ongoing investment and effort on better achieving these outcomes
  • Drive down the effort and cost of achieving these outcomes

We will approach this gradually and iteratively, growing into our transformation aims:

  • Be user-centred: we know who our users are and build our services around their needs
  • Deliver end-to-end: we come together as one department to design and deliver
  • Empower ourselves and others: we feel safe to innovate and take the initiative
  • Make evidence-based decisions: we use our data to make effective decisions and improvements.
